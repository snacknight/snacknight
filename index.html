What’s a Snack Night?

A few years ago we asked the big question: what if we skipped dinner and dedicated a night to our favorite snacks? Full of pickles, tinned fish, and focaccia we asked, what if we did this again? Every month? On the road? In a foreign country?

Snack Night was born.

Around tables of tiny plates, we got to asking more questions like, what if we made a music video? And more documentaries? Videos about our food obsessions? Films that get people talking?

Now, we're a filmmaking team doing just that. Always approaching the story with a little flavor and a lot of intention. Kyle is a director/producer and Irem is an editor/producer. Together we're Snack Night LLC, serving the Washington DC, Maryland, and Virginia (DMV) area from our home base in Arlington and wherever we find stories or snacks.
